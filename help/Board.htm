Game board

In "Treasury" there are three standard boards, everyone following more difficult than the previous.

  • Beginner: The size 9õ9, 15 of jewelry for the sum 32 points.
  • Beginner ¹2: The size 9õ18, 30 of jewelry for the sum 64 points.
  • Beginner ¹3: The size 14õ18, 45 of jewelry for the sum 96 points.
  • Expert: The size 13õ13, 31 of jewelry for the sum 80 points.
  • Expert ¹2: The size 13õ26, 62 of jewelry for the sum 160 points.
  • Expert ¹3: The size 20õ26, 93 of jewelry for the sum 240 points.
  • Professional: The size 18õ18, 63 of jewelry for the sum 196 points.
  • Professional ¹2: The size 18õ36, 126 of jewelry for the sum 392 points.
  • Professional ¹3: The size 27õ36, 189 of jewelry for the sum 588 points.

At game there are 6 types of jewelry. Each appearance has the value.

Nugget

1 points

Amethyst

2 points

Chrysolite

4 points

Pearl

8 points

Sapphire

16 points

Ruby

32 points

To create a board of other size, in the Game click Parameters and choose Special on Size board. The game "Treasury" supports boards from 9õ9 to 36õ72 in size. Total amount of points of all jewelry on a board can't exceed 999 points.

In game 2 modes of game single game and with the computer are provided. For single game choose on Level AI parameter No AI. For game with the computer choose any of the following parameters: Beginner, Expert, Professional, Special.
